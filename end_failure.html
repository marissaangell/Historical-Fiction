FAILURE

The facility’s alarms suddenly start blaring. With a start, your eyes scramble to find the main display. It reads: “Power level rising. Meltdown in progress.”

“No, no, no!” You yell, fumbling for the controls. But it’s too late.

As reactor 3 explodes, you are engulfed in the blast. Just like in 1986, radioactive smoke starts spilling into the sky. Unfortunately, history has been repeated.
